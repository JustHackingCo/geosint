Hello and welcome to Geosint. This main page is where you can find challenges from various competitions. If you are here for a specific competition, make sure you choose challenges from the appropriate category on the left. Newer competitions will show up at the top of the list. I have left up challenges from previous competitions in case anyone wants to give them a crack :)


If you are familiar with GeoGuessr, then you probably already know how to play! If not, feel free to click on a challenge to give it a try.


To submit a guess, click somewhere on the mini-map and hit "Submit". You should receive a yes or no answer depending on if you were correct. "no" means you are incorrect and "yes + flag" means you got it right!
