Directions

Hello and welcome to Geosint. This main page is where you can find challenges.


If you are familiar with GeoGuessr, then you probably already know how to play! If not, feel free to click on a challenge to give it a try.


To submit a guess, click somewhere on the mini-map and hit "Submit". You should receive a yes or no answer depending on if you were correct. "no" means you are incorrect and "yes + flag" means you got it right!
